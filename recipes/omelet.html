Omelette Recipe

An omelette is a dish you might order for breakfast or lunch

For this recipe you will need the following ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Onion (Optional)

Once you've gathered your ingredients, follow the following steps:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and beat with a whisk till runny sprinkle with salt and pepper.

  2. Heat butter in non-stick pan at medium heat till melted.

  3. Pour the eggs into the pan, tilt the pan ever so slightly from one side to another to allow the eggs to swirl and cover the surface of the pan completely. Let the mixture cook for about 20 seconds then scrape a line through the middle with a spatula

  4. Tilt the pan again to allow it to fill back up with the runny egg. Repeat once or twice more until the egg has just set.

  5. At this point, add the onion if you want to add it. Scatter onto the omelette and hold gently in half.

  6. Serve onto a plate and enjoy!
